Our Story

We Put the Perfect Fit First — Always

Founded in 2009 in Aurora, Colorado, Footcap began as a small family-owned shoe boutique with a single mission: to make premium footwear accessible to everyone. What started as a modest storefront has grown into one of North America's most trusted footwear destinations.

We partner directly with the world's leading brands — Nike, Adidas, Puma, Bata, and more — to bring you authentic, quality-guaranteed products at fair prices. Every pair on our shelves and in our online store has been hand-selected by our team of footwear specialists.

Our commitment goes beyond selling shoes. We believe that the right pair of shoes can transform your confidence, performance, and everyday comfort. That's why every member of our team is trained to help you find not just a shoe, but your shoe.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Authenticity

Every product we sell is 100% genuine, sourced directly from authorized brand distributors. No grey market, no counterfeits — ever.

Customer First

Our policies, service hours, and team training all revolve around one thing: making your experience effortless and enjoyable.

Sustainability

We actively choose brands and packaging partners committed to reducing environmental impact — because the planet deserves a good fit too.

Quality Assured

Our in-house QA team inspects every shipment before it reaches our customers. Only the best makes it through our doors.
